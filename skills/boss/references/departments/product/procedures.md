# Product & Discovery procedures

- `ask-matt` — consult external primary-source guidance.
- `create-issue` — refine a requirement into an approved issue.
- `domain-modeling` — sharpen terminology and domain decisions.
- `grill-with-docs` — question requirements against project documents.
- `prototype` — answer a design question with disposable work.
- `research` — produce cited research artifacts.
- `to-spec` — turn discovery into a specification.
- `to-tickets` — split an approved specification into tickets.
- `triage` — produce an agent-ready brief.
- `wayfinder` — chart foggy product territory.
